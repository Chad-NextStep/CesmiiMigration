Education & Workforce Development

Building the Smart Manufacturing workforce of the future

CESMII supports the nation's manufacturing learning infrastructure through the development of extensive and innovative Smart Manufacturing educational resources — educating manufacturing leaders and professionals and upskilling the workforce for adoption of broad-scale smart manufacturing initiatives across the U.S.
