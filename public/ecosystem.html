Affinity Groups

CESMII Affinity Groups connect members who share common interests, industries or technical challenges. These peer-driven communities facilitate knowledge sharing, best practices and collaborative problem-solving.

Groups meet regularly to discuss topics ranging from specific manufacturing sectors to cross-cutting technology themes.

MEP Network Partnership

CESMII has forged a strategic partnership with the NIST Manufacturing Extension Partnership (MEP) to advance U.S. manufacturing competitiveness.

This partnership extends CESMII's reach to small and medium manufacturers nationwide, connecting them with Smart Manufacturing resources, expertise and technology.
