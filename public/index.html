Driving U.S. Leadership in Smart Manufacturing

As the United States’ non-profit institute dedicated to Smart Manufacturing, we are working to reduce cost, complexity, and time-to-value so all manufacturers can engage in Smart Manufacturing. We drive and fund the innovation and R&D necessary to democratize real-time operations data across the entire value chain.

What Is Smart Manufacturing?

Smart Manufacturing is the information-based, event-driven, and collaborative orchestration of business, physical, and digital processes that efficiently drive plants, factories, and the entire value chain.

Ecosystem & Collaboration

Our membership includes manufacturers, machine builders, technology providers, system integrators, and academia — all working together to advance Smart Manufacturing.
