Projects

Driving Smart Manufacturing innovation through funded research

CESMII invests in Smart Manufacturing innovation through projects with funds awarded by the U.S. Department of Energy (DoE). We manage projects in collaboration with our entire ecosystem to develop emerging technologies and drive research innovation — working to democratize Smart Manufacturing.

Project Focus Areas

Research and development across critical manufacturing domains

Enabling Technologies

Developing the foundational technologies that enable broad-scale Smart Manufacturing adoption across industries.

Platform Development

Enhancing the SM Innovation Platform with new capabilities, integrations and standards-based tools.

Data Analytics & AI

Applying data science, machine learning and AI to manufacturing operations for predictive insights and optimization.

Energy Efficiency

Reducing energy consumption and environmental impact through smart, data-driven manufacturing processes.

How Projects Work

CESMII projects are collaborative efforts between member organizations, academic institutions, national labs and the CESMII team. Projects are competitively selected through requests for proposals (RFPs).

Each project addresses a specific manufacturing challenge and delivers tangible outcomes — new tools, validated approaches and reusable knowledge that benefits the broader manufacturing community.
