About CESMII

The Smart Manufacturing Institute

CESMII (pronounced "sez-ME") is a non-profit organization established as your primary advocate for Smart Manufacturing (SM) and your primary source for industry networking, education and workforce development, funded research projects and a Smart Manufacturing Innovation Platform to enable digital transformation.

Funded by the U.S. government as part of the Manufacturing USA network, CESMII accelerates the development and adoption of advanced sensing, data analytics and Smart Manufacturing platform technologies.

Our Mission

To radically accelerate the development and adoption of Smart Manufacturing — ensuring the power of information and innovation is at the fingertips of everyone, including small and medium manufacturers.

What Is Smart Manufacturing?

Smart Manufacturing (SM) is the information-driven, event-driven and collaborative orchestration of business, physical and digital processes that efficiently drive plants, factories and the entire value chain.

Our Focus Areas

SM Innovation Platform (SMIP)

Developing an open, standards-based platform that provides interoperability, open-source tools and standardized data models for manufacturing operations.

Education & Workforce Development

Creating training programs, curricula and hands-on learning opportunities to prepare the workforce for smart manufacturing adoption.

Innovation Projects

Funding and supporting research projects with the U.S. Department of Energy that address critical manufacturing challenges through collaboration between industry, academia and national labs.

Ecosystem & Collaboration

Bringing together manufacturers, machine builders, technology providers, system integrators and academia to advance Smart Manufacturing through collaboration.
